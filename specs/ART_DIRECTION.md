# Visual Direction & Atmosphere

**Theme**: High Traditional Fantasy with a focus on Majesties, Mysteries, and Martial Might.

### 1. Grandeur and Sacred Spaces
The game world should feel ancient and significant. 
- **The Sanctuary (Lobby)**: A mountain-top cathedral of white stone and gold leaf. God-rays, incense, and the sound of distant bells.
- **The Dungeons**: Even in dark places, there should be remnants of lost civilizationsâ€”overgrown ruins, bioluminescent caverns, and massive stone carvings.

### 2. Physicality and Weight
Everything has a physical presence. 
- **Combat**: Heavy swings, metallic clangs, and the sound of stone being crushed.
- **Interaction**: Selecting a mercenary at the **Roster Altar** is an act of spiritual attunement, accompanied by pillars of light and choral swells.
- **HUD**: Minimized and integrated. Use diegetic elements (like the **Tome of Whispers** for abilities) where possible.

### 3. Contrasting Light and Dark
- **Safe Zones (Hubs)**: Warm ambers, bright whites, and lush blues. A sense of peace and preparation.
- **Danger Zones (Missions)**: Inky shadows, flickering torchlight, and the cold glow of hostile magic.
