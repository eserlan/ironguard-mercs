# Tuning & Economy: Gear & Equipment

## Rarity Weights (Base)

| Rarity | Weight | Frequency |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Common | 60% | Guaranteed early; standard filler. |
| Uncommon | 25% | High frequency in mid-run rooms. |
| Rare | 12% | Rare drops from Elites; objective rewards. |
| Exotic | 3% | Boss rewards only; milestone events. |

## Drop Pool Distribution

- **Room Clear (Normal)**: 1 Chest (Common/Uncommon).
- **Elite Defeated**: 1 Guaranteed drop (Uncommon/Rare).
- **Boss Defeated**: 2 Guaranteed drops (Rare/Exotic).
- **Safe Room Merchant**: 3 random items from player's unlocked meta-pool.

## Power Budgets per Tier

- **Common**: single effect, low uptime.
- **Uncommon**: two synergistic effects OR high uptime.
- **Rare**: unique "Mini-Ability" tier effect.
- **Exotic**: transformative mechanic (e.g., "Dash becomes Teleport").
